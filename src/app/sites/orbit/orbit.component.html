THE APP

Orbit is a powerfull and multi-platform tool, with this app you can instantly share any document on the same network with every device.

DETAILS

The main feature of Orbit, is the possibility to evolve from a local network to an open world where your friends or other devices can connect on a “room” and share from anywhere.

COMMUNITY

Manage your friends, your devices, set customs themes with the built-in tools and create the perfect environnement.
